About

Current

I am currently co-founder of Kloodle. We feel that education is a process and not a procession towards end results. We believe that skills are developed on a daily basis and the journey of skill development should be recorded and celebrated. Kloodle is a platform that enables students to record this process and develop their employability skills. We also enable employers to connect with and engage students, increasing awareness about the range of careers available.
